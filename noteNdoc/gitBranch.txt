Branch Naming
	more of guideline than strict rule
	we wouldn't be reading other people's branch name

new feature
	feat/{name}-{date}
		date: month day
	example: feat/sortButton-1231

update excisting feature
	updt/{name}-{date}
	example: updt/indexCss-1120

fix bug
	fix/{name}-{date}
	example: fix/brokenGrid-0430
